I've sent about 20 postcards back to camp up to now. I'll get around to the rest sometime before Christmas (I hope). I'm up to the gills with studying, but heck! I got a lot of time so don't worry, I'll catch up. It so happens that I have 6 more hours of classwork in addition to the 12 I mentioned before. They're 3 hours each for Fluids, and Strength of Materials (just work problems in class). Boy! This because of wearing ties is getting more down. I have to get up about ten minutes earlier just to get it on right which is about 6:45 or 6:50. We have to get into the dining room before 7:25 or we get charged for it. Well adios for a while.

George
