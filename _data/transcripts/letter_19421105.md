Well, I got out 18 more postcards today, also I have to write to everybody? I got a haircut today ($0.65), I'm almost caught up now, I ought to by the end of next week. At these Quaker schools like Swarthmore, Haverford, Bryn Mawr, Penn State they have "collections" every day. At Swarthmore, it is compulsory to attend on Thursdays other days are voluntary. These "collections" are suggested to be more or less religious but on Thursday they have speakers - last week someone spoke on the "founding of Swarthmore College." Today a man named "Humber" who is proposing an international union after the way spoke. I think I lost my "Parker" as I'm using that than red pen. Hope I find it. Today is Nov 5, 1942 9:30 PM.

George
