Nov 17, 1942

Hi everybody!

You know every time I think about it I wonder more how I ever got into Swarthmore. The other night at Dean Hunt's I found out that he goes all over the country interviewing prospective students a couple times each year. Holy shucks! I finished 3 midterms and one more next Monday. Don't expect any extraordinary results, but don't worry about my flunking out - no sir! Not if I can help it. I hope Miyo's still practicing piano, cause it's a good thing to know how to do those things. I sure wish I did. Someday I'll go out to the Cutting Collection and play False Capriciuse Op 47 Dance of the Gnomes and - what's the name of the other one? On the photograph.

Geo.
