Nov. 26, 1942

Hi! I just got back from Mr. Nason's place. We had turkey, borocolli, soup, mashed potatoes, stuffing + pie. I had quite a stomachful, except the part usually filled by rice was still sorta empty. There was a butler serving each course. wowee!! I felt self-concious at first but Mr. Nason + wife made us (Ruth Bonnie, 2 boys + 1 girl (white)) feel at home. We talked afterwards about various subjects until we got to the Relocation Camps + had quite a talk. What surprised me was that Mr. Nason knew about Willie. He said that he was thinking of getting Willie over here except that you're a bio-chem major. I think that was his own idea because he said "Of course the others have to approve too (Dean Hunt.)" I told him that you were willing to be a pre-med. Wonder if anything will come of it? Hope so.

Well this is the first Thanksgiving to my knowledge that we've been separated aint it? Well, God has willed it thus, so lets be thankful anyway. Lets make the best out of this catastrophe the world is in + hope + prepare for better times.

Geo.
