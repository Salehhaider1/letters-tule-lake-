Nov 30, 1942:

Hi! I received the pen today. Thanks; it sure writes good - just the right thickness of line too. I'll try not to lose this one. Even if I do I'll have a better chance of getting it back since my name's on it. I went swimming today for the first time. It sure is strenuous. Its a wonder that I still can keep afloat .. I don't think I've gone swimming since I grew out of the pool in our back yard. The pool is about 100' x 40' + from 5'-9' deep. We go in with our birthday suits on. Dean Hunt wants to know whether Willie wants to come to Swarthmore. He said that about two more fellows could be handled here. I realize that if you can get into a large accredited U, why all the better, but you won't lose anything by coming here I'm sure. The Chem courses are pretty good, but you'll have to change your major won't you

George Snouye
Swarthmore College
