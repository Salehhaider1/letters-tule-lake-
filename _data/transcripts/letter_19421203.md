Dec. 3, 1942

Hi! Shuck, it didn't snow anymore. Today was clear + sunny, but really cold out now! you can still see patches of white in the shade. Well, I finished that book just now. I gotta write a book report on it by tomorrow nite at 10 P.M., but now I'm gonna start on my Fluids homework (its 10:00 PM now): This afternoon I made a voice recording (required of all new students); boy! I had the surprise of my life. I never knew I sounded like that. It kinda reminded me of Harry Mayed's voice Did it used to sound like that to you? Maybe it's changed since I came out here. Anyway, the instructor said that it was very good (I wonder?) except that I pronounced bitten, kitten + mitten as bittn, kittn, + mittn instead of bitten, kit-ten, + mit-ten. I also fooled around with the S.N control booth + played records; practiced changing records + turning them over (there's a technique to it), fading + mixing phono + mikes etc- was a lot of fun. How's every thing? Same as usual? Hope so. Solong.

George
