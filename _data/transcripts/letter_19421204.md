Friday, Dec. 4, 1942

Hi! Not much new today. I spent about 5 hours on the book report. I got the first draft of it + now I'll have to type it out. Its 9:40 PM now so I gotta study for a Strength of Materials Quiz (3hr) Tomorrow. I also spent a couple hours in the library reading The Newsweek Time, + Readers' Digest + the NY Times newspaper. The library has a large no. of current magazines (couple hundred) but no. Radio News, Pop. Mechanics, Pop. Science. It has a lot of technical magazines, + professional magazines as astronomy, religion, economics, banking etc. I mailed a letter (in envelope today) which I stuck in my econ book + forgot. Sorry. Is there any thing new in camp? It sure don't seem like it. About 5 different people wrote to me about snow & the harvest festival & the cabaret + nothing else. Not that I don't appreciate them but it shows how you do get into a rut in camp. and trivial things assume undue importance. such as entertainment + you tend to lose sight of the more important things

Geo.
