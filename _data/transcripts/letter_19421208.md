Mon. Dec. 6, 1942

Hi! Well, I got a C+ on the Econ. Test I took a couple weeks ago it wasn't so bad because nobody got an A, but there's room for improvement. I made up for it on the Strength of Materials quiz I took on Sat. (2 hr) with a 100%! Tonight a former minister of the German (Stressman) Republic spoke on "Germany after the war" over S.N. He seemed to advocate an European Union after the pattern of U.S.A. He recognizes the fact that the various nationalisms + languages would bar a European nation. I think that the question of nationalism + races + languages + color are going to be very ticklish points in the post-war settlement + if everyone doesn't have an open mind + is willing to accept others on an equal basis there's going to be more wars - worse than this one maybe. All these nationalisms which we have + are building up now are going to make things harder because we have to unlearn that much more. He mentioned the mental training + bringing up of the German youth in an atmosphere of national socialism + the problem of re-educating them after the war. I think the American youth will also have to learn to forget such terms as "kill off the whole german or jap race" "you got to learn to date your enemy" + the feeling of superiority of the white race - that is wherever they exist. So long. George
