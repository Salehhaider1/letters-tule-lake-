Thurs. Dec. 17, 1942

Hi! Sorry I missed a few days. I was fairly busy because this was the last week. Had a 3 hour quiz in Fluids yesterday. I guess I did ok., except for some arithmetical errors maybe. Today is the last day of classes + most of the fellows are going home. I guess this place will be sorta lonesome - however, I'm not the only one that's gonna stay here. My plans are still kinda hazy yet. I think I have a job here at school doing carpenter work - ok huh! I'll tell you more later. Last night + today was really cold. The wind cuts right thru your clothes. There's snow on the ground yet too. Willie hear from the Nat. Stud. Rel. Council yet? The school paper yesterday said that the Women's Student Gov't Association is going to sponsor a Japanese student - give a $200 scholarship + the school will add 200 + make a $400 scholarship for tuition. I don't know if that's besides the two Dean Hunt mentioned to me or not - hope not.

solong Geo
